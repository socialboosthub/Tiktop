What is a TikTok Slideshow?

A TikTok slideshow (also known as a photo carousel) is a post format where creators upload multiple images instead of a video. Users can swipe left and right to view the images while an audio track plays in the background.

How to Download Slideshows Using TikTop

Saving a slideshow is incredibly easy with our built-in slider tool. You don't need to install any apps or create an account. Just follow these simple steps:

  1. Open TikTok: Find the photo slideshow you want to download.
  2. Copy the Link: Tap the "Share" arrow icon on the right side of the screen and select "Copy Link".
  3. Paste it in TikTop: Go to the TikTop homepage and paste the link into the download bar, then click the download button.
  4. View the Gallery: TikTop's system will automatically detect that the link is a slideshow and redirect you to our dedicated Slideshow Viewer page.

Understanding the Slideshow Interface

Once TikTop processes your link, you will be taken to a horizontal scrolling gallery. Here is exactly what our tool does to give you the best experience:

  • Horizontal Swipe Gallery: You can swipe left and right to preview all the photos in the TikTok post, just like on the original app.
  • Author Information: Each card displays the creator's avatar, their username (`@username`), and the post's caption so you have the full context of the image.
  • High Quality: Our system communicates with an API to fetch the raw image files directly from TikTok's servers, ensuring maximum quality.

Downloading Individual Images vs. All Images

We built our slideshow tool to give you full control over what you save. On the gallery screen, you will see two main ways to download your photos:

1. Download Specific Images

Under every single photo in the gallery, you will see a button labeled "Download Image X" (where X is the number of the photo). Tapping this button instantly forces the browser to download that specific picture to your camera roll or downloads folder. It will be saved cleanly as a .jpeg file named tiktop-[id]-img-[index].jpeg.

2. Download ALL Images

If you want to save the entire carousel at once, scroll down slightly below the gallery and click the large "DOWNLOAD ALL IMAGES" button.

Note on how "Download All" works: When you click this button, the system triggers the download for each image one by one. We purposely added a 2-second delay between each download. Why? Because web browsers (like Chrome and Safari) will block multiple files from downloading at the exact same time to protect you from spam. This 2-second delay ensures your browser has time to ask you: "Allow this site to download multiple files?" Always click Allow so you get all your pictures!

Are There Any Watermarks?

No! The best part about using TikTop's slideshow downloader is that we extract the pure, raw images. When you take a screenshot on your phone, you get all the TikTok UI buttons (hearts, comments, share arrows) blocking the view. When you use TikTop, you get the pure photo without any TikTok watermarks or UI elements.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why didn't all my images download when I clicked "Download All"?

Your browser's pop-up or multi-download blocker might be active. When you click "Download All", watch out for a browser prompt asking to "Allow downloading multiple files" and accept it.

Can I download the audio from the slideshow?

Our slideshow tool focuses specifically on extracting the high-quality photos. To get the audio, you can paste the link on our main page and choose an MP3 download option if available.

What format are the photos saved in?

The photos are saved in standard .jpeg format, which is perfect for opening on any device, setting as a wallpaper, or sharing with friends.

Is this feature free?

Yes, completely free! You can use our slideshow gallery downloader as many times as you like without any hidden fees.
