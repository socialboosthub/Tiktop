TikTok Algorithm Secrets: Best Times to Post to Go Viral

Published on July 1, 2026 • 6 min read

Creating great content is only half the battle. If you are posting an incredible video at 3:00 AM when your target audience is asleep, the TikTok algorithm will assume the video is bad because of the low initial engagement. Understanding when to post is crucial to hitting the "For You" Page (FYP).

The "Golden Hours" to Post

Based on global engagement data, here are the general best times to post on TikTok (in EST):

  • Tuesday: 2 AM, 4 AM, 9 AM
  • Thursday: 9 AM, 12 PM, 7 PM
  • Friday: 5 AM, 1 PM, 3 PM

Pro Tip: While these are global averages, you should always check your own TikTok Analytics under the "Followers" tab to see when your specific audience is most active.

The Secret Sauce: Completion Rate

Likes and comments are great, but the TikTok algorithm prioritizes one metric above all else: Watch Time (Completion Rate). If a user watches your video all the way to the end, TikTok will push it to 10 more people. If they watch it twice (looping), it gets pushed to 100.

To increase your watch time, make sure your hook (the first 3 seconds) is incredibly engaging. Drop the text on screen immediately so people start reading and stay longer.
