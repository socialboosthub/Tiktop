Legal, Safety & Privacy

7. Is downloading TikTok videos legal?

Downloading videos for personal, offline viewing is generally acceptable. However, redistributing, claiming ownership of, or using the downloaded content for commercial purposes without the creator's explicit permission violates copyright laws. Always respect creators' rights.

8. Will TikTok know if I download a video?

No. When you use a third-party tool like TikTop, you are extracting the video file directly from the public server. The creator and TikTok are not notified.

Troubleshooting

10. Why did my download fail?

The most common reasons for a failed download include: the video is set to private, the video has been deleted by the creator, or the link copied is incorrect. Ensure you are copying the direct link of a public video.

11. Where do the videos save on my iPhone?

If you are using Safari on iOS, downloaded videos typically go to the "Files" app in the "Downloads" folder. You can easily move them to your Photos app from there.
