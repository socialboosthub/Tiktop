The Power of Authenticity

On TikTok, users value real, unfiltered content. As a beginner, you don't need a professional camera. Start by documenting your day-to-day life or explaining a skill you possess.

Leveraging Current Trends

Check the "Discover" tab or your FYP daily. Participating in trending challenges is the fastest way to get your content in front of a new audience.

Finding Your Unique Niche

Don't try to appeal to everyone. Pick a specific interest—be it cooking, coding, or comedy—and stick to it consistently.

Consistency Over Perfection

It is better to post three mediocre videos a week than one "perfect" video a month. The algorithm rewards active creators.

Do I need expensive gear?

No! Most viral videos are shot on modern smartphones. Good lighting and clear audio are more important than an expensive camera.
