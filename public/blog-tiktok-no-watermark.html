However, using the native "Save Video" feature leaves you with a bouncing watermark and often compressed video quality.

If you're wondering how to download a TikTok video without the watermark, you're in the right place. In this guide, we'll walk you through the easiest, fastest, and completely free way to do it using TikTop.

Why Remove the TikTok Watermark?

There are several valid reasons why content creators and everyday users prefer clean videos:

  • Cross-posting: Platforms like Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts algorithmically penalize videos that have the TikTok logo on them.
  • Live Wallpapers: A bouncing watermark ruins the aesthetic of a clean video wallpaper on your phone.
  • Video Editing: Editors need a clean, unbranded source file to make high-quality compilations or reaction videos.

Step-by-Step Guide for iPhone & Android

Removing the watermark takes less than 10 seconds and requires no app installations. Just follow these steps:

Open the TikTok app and locate the video you want to save. On the right side of the screen, tap the "Share" icon (the arrow). Swipe through the menu and tap "Copy Link".

Step 2: Go to TikTop

Step 3: Paste and Process

Step 4: Download
