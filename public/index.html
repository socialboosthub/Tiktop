Understanding the Technology

Many users ask how watermark removal works. When a video is uploaded to TikTok, the platform actually stores two versions: the one you see in the app (with the logo) and a "clean" version stored on their servers. TikTop does not "edit" the video to remove the logo; instead, our script locates the URL of the original, clean file and delivers it directly to your device. This guarantees that there is zero quality loss during the process.

Comprehensive Device Guide

📥 Downloading on Android

Android users have the most seamless experience. Simply open TikTop.online in Chrome, Firefox, or any mobile browser. Once you tap the download button, the file will automatically be saved to your "Downloads" folder. You can access these files via your Gallery app or the "Files" app on your device. Ensure you have granted your browser permission to save files to storage.

🍎 Downloading on iOS (iPhone/iPad)

Due to Apple's security protocols, the process varies slightly depending on your iOS version.
iOS 13 and later: Safari now has a native download manager. When you tap "Download" on TikTop, a prompt will appear asking if you want to download the file. Tap "Download" again, and a small arrow icon will appear in the address bar. Tap that icon to see your video, then save it to your Photos app.
iOS 12 and older: You will need to install a free app called "Documents by Readdle" from the App Store. Open the built-in browser inside that app, navigate to TikTop, and download the video there. You can then move it to your Camera Roll.

💻 Downloading on PC / Desktop

For Windows, Mac, and Linux users, TikTop works instantly without additional software. Copy the link from your browser's address bar (e.g., https://www.tiktok.com/@user/video/...), paste it here, and download. Files usually save to your designated "Downloads" folder. For power users, our "Multi-Download" feature is best used on a desktop, allowing you to copy-paste a list of links from a text file and process them in bulk.
